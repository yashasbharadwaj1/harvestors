    Welcome to Harvester's College

    Our Vision

    The vision of Harvesters Theological College is to equip godly servant-leaders for the Glory of God to be effective Evangelists, Church Planters, Pastors, Missionaries and Teachers in India.

    Our Mission

    To train students to understand and effectively communicate the truth of God’s Revelation (Bible)
    To instill in students a love and passion for the Glory of God
    To cultivate in students a burden for the lost
    To nourish the spiritual life of students through regular chapel services, spiritual formation and mentoring
    To offer the highest quality of distinctive evangelical academic training
    To nurture practical ministry skills in students to be effective evangelists, church planters, pastors, missionaries and teachers
    To encourage involvement in local ministry in the nearby villages
    To foster community among students, staff and professors
