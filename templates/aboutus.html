    Statement of Faith

    We believe there is but one living and true God, creator of all things, infinitely perfect and eternally existing in three persons Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Each is worthy of our worship, trust and obedience We believe that Jesus Christ is true God true man, conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of a virgin. He lived a sinless life and made atonement for our sins by his death on the cross. He arose bodily from the dead and ascended into heaven where, at the right hand of the Father. He is now our High Priest and Advocate. We believe that the Holy Spirit works to glorify the Lord Jesus Christ, convicts the world of sin, righteousness and judgment. The Holy Spirit imparts new life, baptizes the believing sinner into the body of Christ, and permanently indwells all children of God. The Holy Spirit guides, instructs and empowers the believer to live a lifestyle that pleases and glorifies God. He endows believers with spiritual gifts for mutual edification and distinct ministries.

    We believe the Scriptures, both Old and New Testaments, to be inspired Word of God, without error in the original writings, and the supreme and final authority in all matters about which they speak. The believer is to properly interpret and apply the Scriptures through the illuminating work of the Holy Spirit. We believe that man was created in the image of God to glorify and enjoy Him forever. Man fell into sin by a willful act of disobedience to God, and consequently all human beings are born with a sinful nature, commit acts of sin, and are eternally separated from God apart from salvation through Christ. We believe that the substitutionary death of Jesus Christ and His bodily resurrection provides the only ground of justification for all who believe. This salvation is not obtained by human effort or merit, but is entirely a free gift of God’s grace received through faith in Jesus Christ. God Himself will make the faith of all genuine believers persevere to the end, so that no child of God will ever again be lost. We believe that all Christians will yet experience temporary personal sin and spiritual warfare in this life, but from the moment of salvation God has provided the means for overcoming these in the truth of the Scripture, the power of Christ through the indwelling Spirit, and the encouragement of the believers.

    We believe in the personal return of our Lord Jesus Christ to earth in sovereign power and glory to visibly establish His righteousness rule over creation. This blessed hope should vitally affect the daily life and service of the believers. We believe in the bodily resurrection of the dead, of the believer to everlasting blessedness and joy in the presence of God, and of the unbeliever to judgment and everlasting punishment apart from God.

    OUR GOALS

    1. To train students to understand and effectively communicate the truth of God’s Revelation (Bible) .

    2. To instill in students a love and passion for the Glory of God.

    3. To cultivate in students a burden for the lost.

    4. To nourish the spiritual life of students through regular chapel services, spiritual formation and mentoring.

    5. To offer the highest quality of distinctive evangelical academic training.

    6. To nurture practical ministry skills in students to be effective evangelists, church planters, pastors, missionaries and teachers.

    7. To encourage involvement in local ministry in the nearby villages.

    8. To foster community among students, staff and professors.
